Our Alumni

What we learn in this club is something we will use throughout your high school experience, and will continue to use throughout ours lifetime. Our alumni have spread across the country, expanding their knowledge on the passion they discovered in the team. Recently, a graduate was accepted into a SpaceX internship during his sophomore year of college, and will go on to work at Tesla as he pauses his college education. Other alumni have been accepted into Colorado School of Mines, Trinity College in Dublin, and numerous other top engineering schools.
